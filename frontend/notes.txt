To Register a company with Bizlink

Company Name	   Text	        Full legal or brand name of the company
Email Address	   Email	    Official email for communication
Phone Number	Number/Text	    Optional but helpful for B2B users
Website (URL)	Text (URL)	    Company website link (optional but good)
Location	  Dropdown/Text	    City & Country
Industry	     Dropdown	    e.g. Electronics, Textile, Organic, etc.
Tags	       Multiselect	    e.g. Supplier, Exporter, Manufacturer
Company Logo	File Upload	    Small image for branding
About / Desc	Textarea	    Brief intro about the company
Product Types	Multiselect	    What kind of products they offer
Years in Business	Number	    For trust (optional)

Business Registration No.	        For admin verification
Social Links  (LinkedIn/Facebook)	For credibility
Company Size	e.g.            1–10, 11–50 employees
Upload Catalog   (PDF)	        For buyers to download company brochure
